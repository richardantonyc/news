Color

Color has a Power which directly influences our Soul

Red


Red is the color of Energy,Passion and Action.
It excludes Strong and Powerful Energy.
It brings Emotions in us, and Motivates us to take Action.
It is Warm and Positive,associated with Our Physical Needs and Our Will for Survival.

Blue


Blue is the color of Peace and Harmony.
It is the color of Maturity,Rationality,Precision,Capabilities,High values.
It is the color of Trust and Honesty .
It is Reliable and Responsilble and points to Internal Security and Trust.

Green


Green is the color of Balance and Harmony.
It is the color of Growth,Spring,Renewal and Rebirth.
Green promotes Love for nature,Family,Friends,Pets and Home.
It is the color of Stability and Durability.

White


White is the color of Perfection.
It is the color of Charity.
It is the meaning of Purity,Innocence,Fullness and Completion.
White offers Interior cleaning,cleaning of our Thoughts and Emotions

Black


Black is the color of Mystery and unkonown.
It means Protection against External Emotional Stress.
It is Intimidating,Unfriendly and Unapprachable.
It absorbs Negativity.
